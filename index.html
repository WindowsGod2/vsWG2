Mod Information

About

Vs. WindowsGod2 is a Friday Night Funkin' mod created by WindowsGod2 to add lore to his OC with the same name. He figured he would gain more popularity if he made his mod for the game.

History

The first instance of this mod being worked on was on October 7th, 2021, when the first track was made for it. Though, at the time, the mod was not a final decision and was made for fun. Another track was made on October 19th, 2021.
On November 2nd, 2021, a storyline for the possible mod about WindowsGod2 was made. It would have five weeks centered around the idea of depression, and visualizing it with inspiration from We've Destroyed All The Hope In This World. Three days later on November 5th, 2021, two game over tracks were made. Two days after that, another main track would be made.
Five new tracks were made for the mod between November 20th, 2021, and February 7th, 2022. One of those is Reminiscence on December 16th, 2021, the earliest track to be made that currently appears in the mod.
On February 11th, 2022, the discord server for the mod would be created, officially making this mod a reality. From this point forward until March 10th, 2023, the mod would undergo production and hiatuses. Finally, on March 10th, 2023, the official demo of the mod was released on GameBanana, along with its official soundtrack. Between the mod's official birth and demo release, forty-two tracks were made, signifying there will be a lot of content in the upcoming updates.

Version changes

On May 11th, 2024, a minor update to the mod was released as "Demo 1.01" where some assets were changed.
On May 20th, 2024, a second minor update was released as "Demo 1.02". This new update updates Psych Engine 0.7.3, adds new Geometry Dash assets, and Dialogue to the first and only week of the demo.
On June 19th, 2024, a third minor update would be released as "Demo 1.03". This update mainly has bugfixes, more changed assets, new credits, and tweaked dialogue.
On October 6th, 2024, the last minor update to the mod would be released as "Demo 1.04". This update has changes to the note assets, new dialogue sprites for WindowsGod2, more BGM tracks for dialogue cutscenes, tweaks to the dialogue, fixed some asset positions, and marking Week 1 as "easy" in difficulty.

Character Information

WindowsGod2

Appearance

WindowsGod2 is the main character of the mod. He's depicted as the 111th cube icon in Geometry Dash, composed of crimson, violet, and navy blue.
He has two white circles for eyes and a white mouth that changes in shape depending on his expression.
His origins are unknown, but he was first discovered in a container of green fluid and released by accident in January 2012.

Personality

WindowsGod2 is rather energetic and outgoing. Generally, he's friendly and spunky and a bit of a "tsundere" when put in embarrassing situations that can fluster him. He claims not to enjoy physical affection, but this is false.
He is completely aroace, and feels dissatisfaction in romantic situations to the extent that he will not hesitate to act violently in such conditions. This can include verbal, mental, or physical violence.
